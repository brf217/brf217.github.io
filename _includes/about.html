About Me

I've been a professional data scientist since 2016, with deep experience in finance/investing. I started my career in management consulting, but given that I enjoyed the technical side of that career most, I started working toward a more engineering-based role about 5 years in. I started taking programming seriously in 2012 to automate a few repetitive Excel tasks, and enjoyed the process so much, I ended up here.

Given my experience tying analytics directly to high-stakes problems in consulting, I feel comfortable at all stages of data-driven projects. These include everything from gathering requirements, to creation of source data feeds/ETL pipelines, management of data warehouses, visual development in BI tools, and predictive modeling.

My formal education is a B.A. in Economics from New York University and an M.S. in Finance from the University of Colorado, but at this point, I continue to add to my technical knowledge through online courses or projects in new areas.

In summary, I just really enjoy creating a clean, automated process to help my team get the edge they need to outperform the competition.
