About Me

I've been a professional data scientist since 2016, with deep experience in finance/investing. I started my career in management consulting, but given that I only really liked the technical side of that career, I started working toward a more engineering-based role about 5 years in. I began taking programming seriously in 2012 to automate a few repetitive Excel tasks, and enjoyed the process so much, I ended up here

Given my experience with company operations from consulting, and how decisionmaking ties directly to analytics, I feel comfortable at all stages of a project. This includes everything from gathering requirements, to creation of source data feeds/ETL pipelines, management of data warehouses, visual development in BI tools, and predictive modeling.

My formal education is a B.A. in Economics from NYU and an M.S. in Finance from the University of Colorado, but at this point, the most useful knowledge I have has been self-taught.

Now, I just really enjoy creating a clean, automated process to help my team get the edge they need to outperform the competition.
