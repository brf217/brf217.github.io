About

I am a data scientist with deep domain knowledge in finance. Given my experience with many clients in my former role as a management consultant, and my current data science career, I've developed expertise in the full-cycle of analytics projects. This includes everything from gathering requirements, to creation of source data feeds/ETL pipelines, management of data warehouses, visual development in BI tools, and predictive modeling when needed.

My formal education is a B.A. in Economics from NYU and an M.S. in Finance from the University of Colorado. I began programming here and there in 2007 to automate a few repetitive Excel tasks, however, I ended up loving the process so much over time, I went into full-time data science work in 2016.

Mostly, I enjoy creating a clean, automated process to give my team powerful decisionmaking capabilities.
