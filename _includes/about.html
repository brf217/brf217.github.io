About

I am a data scientist with deep domain expertise in the field of finance and investing. Given my various roles, I have developed some expertise in the full-cycle of projects including the creation of source data feeds and ETL pipelines, as well as the management of data warehouses to store my source data.

My formal education is a B.A. in Economics and an M.S. in Finance, but through a demanding career in consulting I started to understand the power of automation, leading me to become a self-taught Python, SQL, and R developer. Initially I did this to become more efficient on analytics projects, however, I ended up loving the process so much I went into full-time data science work in 2016.
